Relax, Explore, Enjoy

Nestled in a beautiful valley in the Swiss Alps, our resort is the perfect escape from the mundane. Dining options include an Italian restaurant and ski-in bars on mountainside terraces. Other amenities consist of an upscale spa, hot tubs, a gym and a climbing wall. Breakfast is available.

Amenities and Options for Everyone

Escape from everyday life and spend a few warm and relaxing days with us. Enjoy the refreshing mountain air and explore the surrounding mountains on foot or with our bikes. Visit our spa or ski runs during winter.

An Escape For Any Time of Year

Whether you prefer a snowy mountain escape or a peaceful summer getaway surrounded by mountains and valleys, we have it all. Ski in winter or hike and swim during the warmer months.
